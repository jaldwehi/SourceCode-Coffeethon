About Coffeethon

Coffeethon 2025 brings together developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and coffee enthusiasts to shape the future of coffee through technology and innovation.

This premier hackathon aims to address challenges in the coffee industry, from farm to cup, by leveraging cutting-edge technology and creative problem-solving. Join us in revolutionizing the world's most beloved beverage.

Problem Statements

Smart Coffee Machines

Develop AI-driven brewing solutions that personalize coffee experiences based on user preferences, weather, time of day, and other contextual factors.

Sustainable Supply Chains

Create transparent systems that track coffee from farm to cup, ensuring fair compensation for farmers and sustainable practices throughout the supply chain.

Enhancing Café Experience

Design digital solutions that transform the coffee shop experience through innovative ordering systems, ambiance control, or community building features.

Tracks

IoT and Hardware Track

Build innovative hardware solutions and IoT devices that enhance coffee brewing, monitoring, or consumption.

Blockchain and Supply Chain

Develop blockchain solutions for transparent, ethical, and efficient coffee supply chains from farm to cup.

Customer Loyalty and Experience

Create digital platforms that transform customer experiences and build loyalty in the coffee industry.
